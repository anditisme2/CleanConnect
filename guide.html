🌿 Wet Waste (Biodegradable)

  • Fruit & Vegetable Peels
  • Leftover Food
  • Egg Shells
  • Flowers
  • Garden Waste

Tip: Use this for composting at home or community compost pits.

🥤 Dry Waste (Recyclable)

  • Plastic Bottles
  • Paper & Cardboard
  • Glass Bottles
  • Metal Cans
  • Rubber & Thermocol

Tip: Rinse containers before disposing. Keep separate from wet waste.
